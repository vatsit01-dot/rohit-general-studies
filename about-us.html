Our Story Starts With One Teacher's Promise

Rohit General Studies was founded by Rohit Sir in Paharpur, Purvi Champaran — with a simple promise: no student gets left behind. What began as a small coaching centre quickly grew into a community of learners who achieved results they never thought possible.

We serve students from Class 1st to 12th, covering all subjects for Bihar Board and CBSE. Our senior students (Class 11 & 12) receive focused coaching for the Arts stream, preparing them not just for board exams but for life.

Beyond academics, we prepare students for Medha chatravriti Scholarship, Navodaya, Sainik School, and general competitive exams — because we believe every child should aim higher.
